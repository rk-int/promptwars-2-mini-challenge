# promptwars-2-mini-challenge

🎯 The Objective

Create an AI image that perfectly captures the spirit, culture, or unique identity of a state.

⚠️ CRITICAL RULE: 
You must not mention or write the state name anywhere in your generation prompt, inside the image text, or in your social copy. Let the visuals do the talking!

🌶️ Pick Your Creative Angle

🚫 No Buildings Allowed: Show your state without monuments or architecture. 

🎬 The Movie Poster: Reimagine your state's vibe as a blockbuster film.

👤 The Chosen One: Turn your state's soul into a legendary hero or character. 

🍲 Strictly Culinary: Tell your state's story using only food and ingredients.

🚀 Fast Forward to 2050: Imagine your state placed inside a futuristic world. 

🐉 The Spirit Animal: If your state were a living creature, what would it look like?
